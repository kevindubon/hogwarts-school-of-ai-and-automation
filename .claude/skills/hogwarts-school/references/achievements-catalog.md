# Hogwarts School of AI and Automation — Achievements Catalog

> *"It is our choices that show what we truly are, far more than our abilities."*
> — Albus Dumbledore (and also your achievement log)

This catalog defines every badge and achievement a student can earn throughout their journey at the Hogwarts School of AI and Automation. Each achievement has a unique ID, unlock condition, point bonus, and a description worthy of the Daily Prophet.

---

## Milestone Achievements

These achievements are awarded when a student accumulates a certain number of house points. The Great Hall scoreboard updates accordingly.

---

### prefect
**Name:** Prefect
**Badge:** `[P]`
**Unlock Condition:** Reach 100 house points
**Description:** You've been entrusted with the Prefect badge — a shiny token of your dedication and a license to patrol the corridors of automation. Don't let it go to your head... but also, feel free to dock points from Peeves.
**Points Bonus:** +10

---

### head_of_house
**Name:** Head of House
**Badge:** `[HoH]`
**Unlock Condition:** Reach 500 house points
**Description:** Five hundred points! Even Dumbledore is raising an eyebrow. You've demonstrated mastery that would make any Head of House weep with pride. Your portrait will hang in the common room — right next to the fireplace, where it's warmest.
**Points Bonus:** +25

---

### order_of_merlin
**Name:** Order of Merlin
**Badge:** `[OoM]`
**Unlock Condition:** Reach 1000 house points
**Description:** One THOUSAND points. Merlin himself would be impressed, and he literally has an Order named after him. You've joined the rarest echelon of wizards — those who didn't just learn the spells, but rewrote the spellbook. The Ministry of Magic is sending an owl. Several owls, actually.
**Points Bonus:** +50

---

## Module Completion Achievements

Awarded upon completing each module's requirements (quizzes passed and exercises done). Each one marks a milestone in your magical education.

---

### sorted
**Name:** The Sorting
**Badge:** `[SORTED]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete the Sorting Ceremony (Module 0)
**Description:** The Sorting Hat has spoken! After a brief (and surprisingly philosophical) conversation atop your head, you've been placed into your house. The hat considered putting you in all four houses, but that would break the database schema. Welcome to Hogwarts, young wizard.
**Points Bonus:** None

---

### first_spell
**Name:** First Spell Cast
**Badge:** `[FS]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete Module 1 (Automation Fundamentals)
**Description:** "Wingardium Leviosa!" — except instead of a feather, you just levitated an entire automation workflow. Professor Flitwick would be proud (and slightly concerned about the ceiling tiles). You've mastered the fundamentals, and there's no going back to doing things manually.
**Points Bonus:** +5

---

### potions_master
**Name:** Potions Master
**Badge:** `[PM]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete Module 2 (Data Structures)
**Description:** You've brewed the perfect potion of arrays, objects, and nested data structures. Professor Snape grudgingly admits your JSON formatting is... acceptable. Unlike actual potions class, nothing exploded, no cauldrons melted, and you didn't accidentally turn anyone into a newt.
**Points Bonus:** +5

---

### owl_handler
**Name:** Owl Handler
**Badge:** `[OH]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete Module 3 (APIs)
**Description:** You now speak fluent Owl Post — also known as REST APIs. GET, POST, PUT, DELETE... you handle HTTP methods the way Hagrid handles magical creatures: with enthusiasm and surprisingly few injuries. The Owlery has never been more efficient.
**Points Bonus:** +5

---

### wand_ready
**Name:** Wand Ready
**Badge:** `[WR]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete Module 4 (Workspace Setup)
**Description:** "The wand chooses the wizard, Mr. Potter." And YOUR wand just chose a fully configured workspace with all the right tools installed. Ollivander would charge 7 Galleons for this setup, but you built it yourself. Holly and phoenix feather? No — VS Code and CLI.
**Points Bonus:** +5

---

### cartographer
**Name:** Cartographer of Connections
**Badge:** `[CC]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete Module 5 (MCPs)
**Description:** "I solemnly swear that I am up to no good." You've mapped every MCP server the way the Marauders mapped Hogwarts — except your map connects APIs, databases, and services instead of secret passageways. Although, technically, MCPs ARE secret passageways between systems. Mischief managed.
**Points Bonus:** +5

---

### spell_creator
**Name:** Spell Creator
**Badge:** `[SC]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete Module 6 (Claude Skills)
**Description:** Most wizards learn spells. You INVENTED one. Like Snape creating Sectumsempra (but, you know, for good), you've forged a Claude Skill from raw markdown and determination. The Half-Blood Prince's textbook has nothing on your `.claude/skills/` directory.
**Points Bonus:** +5

---

### champion
**Name:** Triwizard Champion
**Badge:** `[TC]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete Module 7 (Final Trial)
**Description:** You've survived the Final Trial — a gauntlet worthy of the Triwizard Tournament. Dragons? Check (they were API rate limits). The Black Lake? Check (deep-diving into nested data). The maze? Check (debugging production workflows at midnight). The Goblet of Fire chose wisely.
**Points Bonus:** +15

---

### dark_arts_defense
**Name:** Defense Against the Dark Arts
**Badge:** `[DADA]`
**Unlock Condition:** Complete Module 8 (Prompt Engineering)
**Description:** Unlike the cursed DADA teaching position, YOUR knowledge of prompt engineering will last longer than one year. You've learned to defend against vague outputs, hallucinated data, and the darkest art of all — poorly structured prompts. Expecto Patronum, indeed.
